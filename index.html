Hello I'm Danielle, a passionate Web Designer and Developer.

I graduated from Ball State University with a Bachelor's in Japanese and minors in Computer Technology and Asian Studies. I then ventured into web development through Eleven Fifty Academy's accelerated program. After completion I began teaching there as well.

Teaching at Eleven Fifty Academy, I've had the pleasure of guiding countless students through HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and more. I thrive on problem-solving and critical thinking, always eager to learn and share knowledge.

Beyond coding, I enjoy gardening, gaming, and reading. With a diverse skill set and a love for learning, I'm committed to making a positive impact wherever I go.
